# 🛒 Instacart EDA Analysis  
**Exploratory Data Analysis • Customer Behavior Insights • Data Cleaning • Visualization • Python**

---

## 📌 Project Overview  
This project analyzes Instacart grocery order data to uncover insights about **customer purchasing behavior**, product demand, and ordering patterns.  
The dataset is a modified version of Instacart’s 2017 Kaggle competition data.

The mission in the project was to:
- Clean and prepare the data  
- Address missing and duplicate values  
- Analyze ordering behavior across customers  
- Produce visualizations that help understand Instacart's customer habits

---

## 🗂️ Dataset Description

The dataset is based on Instacart’s 2017 online grocery shopping data and includes:

- 🧾 Orders dataset: Customer order history and timestamps  
- 🛍️ Products dataset: Product names and categories  
- 🏬 Departments & Aisles: Product classification hierarchy  
- 🔁 Order Products: Details on items within each order  

The data represents thousands of customers and millions of grocery transactions.

## 💼 Business Impact

This analysis provides actionable insights that can directly support business decisions:

- 📈 **Marketing Optimization:** Identifies high demand products to target promotions and increase conversions  
- 🛒 **Inventory Planning:** Helps ensure top selling items are always in stock  
- ⏰ **Operational Efficiency:** Reveals peak order times to optimize staffing and delivery logistics  
- 🔁 **Customer Retention:** Highlights reorder behavior to support personalized recommendations  

These insights simulate real world data driven decision making used by companies like Instacart.

## 🎯 Business Questions  
The analysis focuses on answering key business questions such as:

- **Which products are purchased most often?**  
- **What are the busiest hours and days for orders?**  
- **How frequently do customers reorder the same items?**  
- **Which departments drive the most orders?**  
- **What patterns reveal customer behavior and product demand?**

These insights help optimize marketing, inventory planning, and operational decision making.

## 📊 Key Analysis & Insights  

### 🥑 1. Most Ordered Products
- Identified top performing products and categories driving the highest order volume  
- Produce and dairy consistently ranked as the most purchased departments  
- Indicates strong demand for essential grocery items  

### ⏰ 2. Order Timing Patterns
- Peak ordering occurs during evening hours  
- Weekends show increased and more varied ordering behavior compared to weekdays  
- Suggests opportunity for targeted promotions during high traffic periods  

### 🔁 3. Reorder Behavior
- A significant portion of orders are repeat purchases  
- Indicates strong customer loyalty and predictable buying habits  
- Opportunity to build recommendation systems or subscription models  

### 🏬 4. Top Departments
- Produce and dairy dominate both order volume and reorder frequency  
- Suggests these categories are critical for customer retention and satisfaction
